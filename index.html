The story of us.

We have become such a better version of ourselves and I want to thank God for you. Your life was used in mine to become a great encouragement. You became my supportive partner in this journey. He made our fates intertwine and placed us right where we needed to be. Meeting you is one of my greatest blessing.

Enchanted.

It is not simply your appearance that makes you beautiful, it is your kind soul that treats those around you with genuine care and inspiring words. You are sincerely one of a kind.

Meeting you was enchanting enough but having you until the end of the road is a surreal blessing from above. This does not mark the end of our journey but we are simply opening a new chapter. Sparks fly.

You made my heart jump for joy whenever I see you laugh and smile, sparks flew instantly.

My gratitude is all for Him.

I really thank God for you. You became my place of safety, a non-judgmental (maybe) listener, my partner in faith and prayer, what more can I ask for? As I prayed for that special Godsend, I found that my prayer was already answered.
