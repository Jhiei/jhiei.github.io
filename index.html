To the "Joy" that gave me joy,

It was worth every second of waiting.
This is the voice of my heart.

In amazement of your beauty.

People say beauty is in the eye of the beholder. As the beholder, I found truth when I became struck to the beauty I witnessed, you. It's almost been a year ever since we started talking and so many seasons have passed, yet I still feel that time halts in every amazement of you.

The beauty of your soul left me rendered speechless along with your genuine emotions, your kindness, and most especially… your faith in the most loving Father. The way you convey the fire of your faith is an inspiration to me and many others. You are one of a kind.

The commitment that led us closer to Him.

You have encouraged me more than you think. More than I originally thought, honestly. Our almost-a-year of devotions has been a big part of me this past year and I want to thank you for being part of my journey of being closer to Him.

There are so many more devotions we’ll encounter together and I am sure that as you sink deeper in your faith, God will move in ways you have never imagined. Same goes for me, of course. Cheers to more consistent weeks!

Indeed, slow is the quicker path.

As you fulfil every role that life brings, I want to remind you to take everything as slow as you need it to be. You don’t have to cope with the speed of everything revolving around you, you are free to take your time. Take it one step at a time and you’ll find that indeed, being slow does not equate to being last.

"Dear dreamer, slow is fast." ~ Joena San Diego

My gratitude is all for Him.

Truly it is all thanks to Him that you have successfully entered my life. My heart, even. I believe it was God who led us to this very moment and made our paths intertwine so that we can help each other grow not just at surface level but for a well-rooted foundation of faith as we pursue Him.

I really thank God for you. You became my place of safety, a non-judgmental (maybe) listener, my partner in faith and prayer, what more can I ask for? As I prayed for that special partner, I found that my prayer has already been answered.

God will always be my first.

I may have been too nervous to speak but I want to tell you that God will always come first. He will always be my number one priority whatever the odds. And it goes without saying, I know I won’t be the first as well, as it should be.

I will court you and lead our friendship deeper in the way that honours the Lord. I cannot promise to be the most perfect suitor, I will make mistakes but I promise to attempt everything with my best. You will always be a part of my prayers.

With courage, I am ready...

I can never be perfect but I can be the most suitable and as He prepares me, may the Lord also prepare you to the most perfect version of yourself. It is in my heart and prayers for everything to work as God intends. It can be quite overwhelming and I know you want to be fully ready first.

I want to tell you that I am also in the same season of preparation. If it is in God’s will for me to be your first, I am ready to wait for the timing of the only One perfect. I will give it my all to be patient and to allow myself to be led by the Holy Spirit. You can rest assured that as the time you prepare yourself, I will be right there in the waiting as your cheerleader cheering you on. I will willingly wait for you.

Praying I made this day more special, at least a little.

So, welcome to another chapter. I declare that you will not simply receive the desires of your heart but even greater than what you asked for. May the Lord open the heavens and all the blessings flow upon you and your loved ones.

Anica Joy, once again, thank you for being a part of my life. Blessed and happiest birthday to you!

Yours, Jimmi ❤️
