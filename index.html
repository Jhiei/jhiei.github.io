The story of us.

It's truly so difficult to believe that we have made it this far. If you think about it, our story began with a simple scenery but it turned out to be the beginning of a God-filled friendship. Isn't it just too good to be true?

Throughout the journey, we encountered many challenges that brought spiritual growth to us. We bravely battled through prayers, through faith and trust that God will carry us through it. We have become such a better version of ourselves and I want to thank God for you. Your life was used in mine to become a great encouragement. You became my supportive partner in this journey. He made our fates intertwine and placed us right where we needed to be. Meeting you is one of my greatest blessing.

Enchanted.

I cannot deny how enchanting it was to meet you. I'm wonderstruck to the beauty you have displayed. Most especially the way you glorify Him with your faith, it is an art to be admired. It is not simply your appearance that makes you beautiful, it is your kind soul that treats those around you with genuine care and inspiring words. You are sincerely one of a kind.

Meeting you was enchanting enough but having you until the end of the road is a surreal blessing from above. This does not mark the end of our journey but we are simply opening a new chapter. There are so many more devotions and leadings of the Holy Spirit that we will encounter together and I am sure that as we both sink deeper in our faith through His grace, He will move in ways we have never imagined.

Sparks fly.

I don't know how obvious I have shown that I like you not just as a friend, but as someone more special. You have become my place of safety, my favourite person to pray for, that someone who is always ready to listen to my non-stop stories and unfunny jokes until past midnight. Though unfunny, I'm sure you giggled even just a little. Maybe? But I'm thankful you even listened in the first place. You made my heart jump for joy whenever I see you laugh and smile, sparks flew instantly.

My gratitude is all for Him.

Truly it is all thanks to Him that you have successfully entered my life. My heart, even. I believe it was God who led us to this very moment and made our paths cross so we can help each other grow a well-rooted foundation of faith as we pursue His own heart.

What more can I ask the Lord? He gave me a gift that is worth more than any treasure. Truly my gratitude is all for Him. I surrendered myself to His will and let God move in behalf of me and He did not fail. He introduced me to you. As I prayed for that special gift of God, I found that it was you, my non-judgy listener (maybe), was the answer to my year-long prayer.

This always goes without saying. He brought us to it, let Him bring us through it.
